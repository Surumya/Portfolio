# Case Study: Zepto Average Order Value (AOV) Optimization Strategy

**Role:** Product Manager (Analysis by Surumya Sharma)
**Objective:** Drive unit economics profitability by increasing Average Order Value (AOV) from ₹500 towards the ₹650+ benchmark.

## Executive Summary
Zepto commands high volume but suffers from lower AOV compared to Blinkit/Swiggy. This study identifies high-intent user segments and proposes a **Progressive Cart Milestone** framework to increase basket size by 15% through gamified value perception.

## 1. Problem Statement (SEO: Unit Economics, Q-Comm)
Zepto's current AOV (~₹500) trails market leaders (~₹650-700). For a quick-commerce player, low AOV results in strained delivery margins. The challenge: Increase basket size without hurting Zepto's core "speed" perception.

## 2. User Research & Insights (Segmentation)
- **Deal-Driven Value Seekers:** Motivated by discounts; drops off after reaching free delivery.
- **Impulse/Convenience Users:** High urgency, low browsing; adds only what is strictly needed.
- **The Insight:** Users view free delivery as an *endpoint* rather than a *checkpoint* for further value.

## 3. Frameworks Used: RICE & JTBD
- **RICE:** To prioritize feature impact on AOV vs development effort.
- **Jobs To Be Done (JTBD):** Understanding the emotional triggers of a "Pantry Stock-up" vs "Emergency Top-up."

## 4. Proposed Solution: Progressive Cart Milestones
> **Core Epic:** Transform the cart into a value-discovery journey.

- **Milestone 1 (₹199):** Free Delivery unlocked.
- **Milestone 2 (₹399):** "Save ₹25 more" on specific pantry essentials.
- **Milestone 3 (₹599):** Loyalty point multipliers or Zepto Pass trial.

## 5. Implementation Roadmap (RICE Matrix)

| Solution | Reach | Impact | Confidence | Effort | RICE Score |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Progressive Cart Milestones** | 90% | 1.3 | 90% | 2 (Med) | **52.65** |
| Smart Companion Suggestions | 70% | 1.3 | 80% | 3 (High) | 24.27 |
| Threshold Discounts | 80% | 1.1 | 75% | 1 (Low) | 66.00 |

## 6. The "North Star" & KPIs
- **North Star:** Average Order Value (AOV) Target > ₹600.
- **KPIs:**
    - % Conversion from Milestone 1 to Milestone 2.
    - Category Diversity (Avg. # of categories per order).
    - Contribution Margin per Order.
