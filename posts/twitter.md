# Project: Twitter (X) Growth Strategy: Mapping & Optimizing the AARRR Funnel

**Role:** Product Manager (Case Study)
**Objective:** Identify growth opportunities by analyzing user friction points and proposing high-impact Epics prioritized via the RICE framework.

## 1. Problem Statement
Twitter (X) faces a "discovery friction" problem where logged-out users or new sign-ups often drop off before reaching their "Aha! moment." The goal was to analyze the user journey and convert "lurkers" into "active participants."

## 2. The Framework: AARRR Funnel Analysis
- **Acquisition:** Entry via shared links/SEO. Landing on high-value threads.
- **Activation:** Reaching the "First relevant tweet" within 60-120 seconds.
- **Retention:** Daily habits formed through the "For You" feed and notifications.
- **Revenue:** Transitioning users to Premium/X-Subscribed models.
- **Referral:** Viral loops through quote-tweets and external sharing.

## 3. Key Insights & Gaps Identified
- **The Friction Gap:** Login walls kill discovery for non-users.
- **The Onboarding Gap:** Interest selection is often skipped, leading to a "cold start" feed.
- **The Engagement Gap:** High "social cost" for passive users to reply/like.

## 4. Proposed Solutions (Epics)
- **Epic 1: Guest Mode (The Open Viral Loop):** Allow unlimited reading of threads for non-users. Implement "Soft Signup" only upon interaction.
- **Epic 2: Instant Interest Graph:** Use referral source data to auto-populate the feed immediately.
- **Epic 3: Lightweight Reactions:** Introduce nuanced reactions (like Slack/LinkedIn) to lower the barrier for engagement.

## 5. Prioritization (RICE Matrix)
| Epic | Reach | Impact | Confidence | Effort | RICE Score |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Guest Mode | 10/10 | High | 80% | Med | High |
| Instant Interest | 8/10 | Med | 70% | High | Med |
| Reactions | 9/10 | Med | 90% | Low | High |

## 6. Success Metrics (KPIs)
- **North Star:** Daily Active Minutes per User.
- **Activation:** % of new users who follow 5+ accounts in first 24h.
- **Growth:** Viral Coefficient (k-factor) from shared Guest Mode threads.
